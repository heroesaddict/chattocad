Our company information

We Create, Design and Make it Real

ChattoCAD is a professionally managed Solidworks Drafting and Modeling services and outsourcing company with remote offices in Cebu and Bohol, Philippines.

We are highly skilled and experienced Solidworks professionals and has completed hundreds of projects ranging from mechanical, structural and product designs with high rating and great client feedback.

Our Skills

Solidworks is our primary modeling and drafting software but we also use Autocad and Rhino as per client's request.
We use Keyshot for our rendering services.
